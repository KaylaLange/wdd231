Final Project Siteplan

Website Name: Vitality Vibes

This name represents a website that is focused on providing information on health and wellness and supports vitality.

Site Purpose: for users seeking information on diet, exercise, and mental health tips to improve their vitality.

This site provides resources for users to improve their health through a holistic approach through diet and meal recommendations, exercise routines, and practices that improve mental/emotional health.

Scenarios: for users seeking information on diet, exercise, and mental health tips to improve their vitality.

  • How can I improve my stress levels?
  • What exercises can I do with injured knees?

Color Schema:

  • Primary Color: #3D4F42 to be used for navigation bar and throughout site
  • Secondary Color: #F6B389 to be used for buttons and other elements throughout site
  • Accent Color: #F7A637 to be used as an accent throughout site to provide variety and depth

Typography:

  • Heading Font:Granville
  • Paragraph Font: Inter
